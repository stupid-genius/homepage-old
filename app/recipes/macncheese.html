Mac -N- Cheese a la Ng

  • 1-2 boxes maccaroni and cheese
  • 1 Tbs. butter/margarine
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1-3 oz. ground hambuger
  • garlic, onion, curry powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  1. Brown and crumble hamburger; season with garlic/onion/curry powders.
    note: if you brown more at a time (e.g. 1 lb.), it will keep well in the fridge.
  2. In medium saucepan, bring water to boil and add pasta; drain.
  3. Add butter, cheese powder, milk; stir.
  4. Add hamburger and tomato sauce; stir.

Spicey Chicken Casserole

  • 1 lb. chicken
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup (8 oz.) melted margarine
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 stack Ritz crackers
  1. Boil a chicken and debone; cut into pieces. (or cut and pan fry chicken breast)
  2. Mix with 2 cans cream of chicken soup, 8 oz. sour cream, 1 cup cheddar cheese, 1/2 teaspoon cayene pepper, 1/2 cup (4 oz.) melted margarine.
  3. Boil 1 cup rice and put in bottom of 13 x 9 greased cassarole pan. Pour chicken mixture over rice.
  4. Grate 1/2 cup cheddar cheese on top of cassarole.
  5. Crush 1 stack of Ritz crackers and mix with 1/2 cup (4 oz.) melted margerine. Spread over cassarole.
  6. Bake at 375 for 25 min.

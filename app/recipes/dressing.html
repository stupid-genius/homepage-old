Dressing

  • 1 box of cornbread mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tab. sage
  • 1 Tab. soy sauce
  • chicken broth
  1. make the cornbread according to directions on box
  2. let it cool, then crumble it into a bowl
  3. add 2 eggs, the chopped onion, and all seasonings; mix well
  4. add enough broth to moisten the mixture
  5. preheat oven to same temp. used to cook cornbread
  6. grease pan
  7. bake till brown

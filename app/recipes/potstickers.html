Fried Dumplings Peiping Style

Dough

  • 3 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup warm boiled water

Mix flour with 3/4 cup of water, stir and knead into dough. Leave it for 30 min.

Filling

  • 6 oz. ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 teasp. salt
  • 1 Table. soy sauce
  • 3 green onions
  • 1"^3 ginger
  • 1 Table. sesame oil

Chop (fine) and mix all ingredients.

  1. Roll out dough into long roll and cut it into 40 - 50 pieces. Flatten each with hand and roll into round thin pancake, put meat mixture in center then fold over to make a half circle and pinch edges together.
    note: do not let the filling touch the edges of the wrapper or it will not stick together.
  2. Heat pan until very hot, add 2 Table. oil, when oil is hot, add enough dumplings to cover the bottom of the pan without overlapping, saute' over medium heat until bottom is golden.
  3. Turn to low heat, add 2/3 cup water. Cover and cook until water has evaporated.
  4. Place serving plate over pan and invert to serve.

Ramen Noodles Ng Style

  • 1-2 packages Ramen noodles
  • 1-3 oz. ground hambuger
  • 1 Tbs. soy sauce
  • 1 tsp. seasoned sesame seed oil
  • garlic, onion, curry powder
  1. Brown and crumble hamburger; season with garlic/onion/curry powders.
    note: if you brown more at a time (e.g. 1 lb.), it will keep well in the fridge.
  2. In medium saucepan, bring water to boil and add hamburger.
  3. Add noodles and cook until soft; drain.
  4. Add soy sauce, sesame seed oil and half a packet of Ramen noodle seasoning.

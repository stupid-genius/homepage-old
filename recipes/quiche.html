Quiche

  • 1 deep dish pie shell
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded swiss cheese
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • minced onion
  1. cook and crumble bacon into bottom of shell
  2. beat eggs and mix with salt, cayenne, and milk
  3. toss swiss cheese in the flour and mix with the onion; add to egg mixture
  4. pour into the shell
  5. cook; then let stand 10 minutes before serving.

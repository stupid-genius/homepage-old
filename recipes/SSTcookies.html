Cookie Monster's Sugar Cookie Dough

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  1. Incorporate the sugar into the butter
  2. Incorporate the eggs and vanilla
  3. Incorporate flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Chill for one hour
